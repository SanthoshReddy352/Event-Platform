# EventX — User Guide

## Overview
This guide provides participants with all the necessary information to navigate the EventX platform, from browsing events and registering to managing their accounts and getting support.

---

## Quick Start (for Participants)
Follow these simple steps to start engaging with events on EventX:

1.  **Browse Events**: Visit the homepage or navigate to the `Events` section to view upcoming hackathons and tech events.
2.  **Browse by Club**: You can also filter events by the club that posted them to find events from your favorite organizations.
3.  **Select Event**: Click on an event card to view its full details, including description, schedule, and venue.
4.  **Register**: If registration is open, click the `Register` button to access the event's custom registration form.
5.  **Complete Form**: Fill out all required fields accurately and upload any necessary documents.
6.  **Submit Registration**: Review your submission and click `Submit`.
7.  **Payment (If Applicable)**: If the event requires a fee, you will be redirected to a payment gateway (e.g., Razorpay) to complete the transaction. A payment confirmation will be displayed upon success.
8.  **Await Approval**: Some events require manual approval. You will receive an email notification regarding the status of your registration (pending, approved, or rejected).

---

## Account Management
Managing your profile and registrations is straightforward:

-   **Sign Up / Login**: Create a new account or log in using your email address and password.
-   **Edit Profile**: Access your profile settings via `My Account` → `Profile` to update your personal information (e.g., name, phone number).
-   **View My Events**: Keep track of all events you have registered for by navigating to `My Account` → `My Events`. Here you can see your registration status and details.

---

## Registration Flow Details
A more in-depth look at the registration process:

1.  **Event Page Access**: Navigate to the specific event page you are interested in.
2.  **Initiate Registration**: Click the `Register` button, which will display the event-specific registration form.
3.  **Form Completion**:
    -   Carefully fill in all required text, email, number, URL, date, and text area fields.
    -   Select appropriate options from dropdowns and check any relevant checkboxes.
    -   If file uploads are requested, ensure files meet the specified type and size requirements.
4.  **Payment (if required)**: If the event has a registration fee, you will be prompted to complete the payment via a secure gateway. Your registration will only be considered complete after successful payment.
5.  **Confirmation**: Upon successful submission, you will receive an on-screen confirmation and potentially an initial email acknowledging receipt of your application.
6.  **Approval Process**: For events requiring approval, your application status will initially be `Pending`. You will receive a separate email once an administrator has reviewed and updated your status to `Approved` or `Rejected`.

---

## Cancelling Registration
If you need to cancel your registration for an event:

-   Go to `My Account` → `My Events`.
-   Select the registration you wish to cancel.
-   Click the `Cancel` button.
-   **Refunds**: Any refunds for paid events are handled by the event administrators through the platform's payment dashboard. Refund eligibility and processing are subject to the event's specific cancellation and refund policy.

---

## Help & Support
If you encounter any issues or have questions:

-   **Contact Event Organizers**: For event-specific inquiries, refer to the contact information provided on the individual event page.
-   **General Support**: For platform-wide assistance or technical issues, please contact `support@eventx.com`.
